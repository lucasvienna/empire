### Storage for Food/Wood

For each keep level, using the food (or wood) generation values:

• Total capacity = 2 × (generator capacity) × (building cap)  
• Keep capacity = Total capacity ÷ 3  
• Warehouse capacity = Total capacity × 2⁄3
